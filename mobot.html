Mobot Races

The Robotics Institute will celebrate National Robotics Week starting on Thursday, April 12th with the Teruko Yata Memorial Lecture and will conclude on Friday, April 20th with project demonstrations, lab tours, MOBOT Races, and a reception for current and former Robotics Institute Alumni.

Please click here for updates to the Schedule of Events

If pre-registration is required, please RSVP here by Monday, April 17.
