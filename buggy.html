Spring Carnival 2012:
As Seen On TV
April 19th to 21st

Buggy

Sweepstakes, also known as Buggy, is held on Friday and Saturday of Spring Carnival. Greek and Independent organizations race with their buggies, small, low, aerodynamic vehicles, powered only by gravity and human pushers. At its fastest, a buggy can reach speeds up to 35 miles per hour.

For more information, please visit the Buggy Alumni Association website.
