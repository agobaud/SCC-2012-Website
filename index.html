Spring Carnival 2012:
As Seen On TV

Come join the fun!

The Carnegie Mellon University Spring Carnival is the biggest annual event in the school year. Student organizations across campus, whether independent or Greek, work very hard to succeed in uniquely competitive events featured during the three-day period.

Advance Sale Ride Tickets are going on sale at the UC Information Desk starting April 2nd - for $10, you get a strip of 6 tickets, each of which are good for one ride each!

Contact Us!

For more information about Spring Carnival 2012, please email us at sc0v@andrew.cmu.edu. Otherwise, please visit our Committee page to get a list of our committee members!


Carnegie Mellon University's Spring Carnival is funded in part by your Student Activities Fee.
